01

Introduction

Welcome to PawPass – Digital Pet Passport. PawPass is a comprehensive mobile app that helps pet owners manage their pets' health records, vaccinations, medications, appointments, weight history, and veterinary documents — all in one place.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. By creating an account or using the App, you confirm you are at least 13 years old and agree to the practices described here.

03

Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide PawPass's services:

  • Account data: your email address and password, managed securely via Supabase Auth.
  • Pet profiles: name, breed, age, weight, photos, and medical notes you choose to enter.
  • Health records: vet visit details, diagnoses, treatments, costs, vaccination records, medications, and appointments.
  • Uploaded documents: images and PDF files you upload (e.g. vet certificates), stored in Supabase Storage.
  • Device permissions: camera / photo library (for pet photos and documents), push notification tokens (for appointment reminders), and file storage (for document export and PDF generation).
  • Usage data: anonymised crash reports and diagnostics to improve app stability.

We do not collect data that isn't needed for the app to function. We do not track your location, read your contacts, or access any data outside of what PawPass explicitly requests permission for.

04

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve PawPass:

  • To create and manage your account and pet profiles.
  • To display your health records, vaccinations, medications, and appointments.
  • To send push notifications for upcoming appointments or vaccine due dates (only if you grant permission).
  • To process your subscription via in-app purchases — handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • To generate PDF exports of your pet's health records.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance using anonymised diagnostics.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers. Your data is shared only with:

  • Supabase — our backend provider for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Supabase acts as a data processor under our instructions and is contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Apple / Google — for processing in-app purchases. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Crash-reporting tools — anonymised diagnostics only; no personally identifiable information is included.

PawPass contains no advertising. No ad networks, no tracking SDKs, no data brokers. Your pet's health data stays yours.

07

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase-hosted servers with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We apply industry-standard security practices. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password for your PawPass account.

Uploaded documents (images, PDFs) are stored in Supabase Storage and are private to your account. They are not publicly accessible.

08

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data — including pet records, health history, and uploaded documents — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at the email below.

09

Children's Privacy

PawPass is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Users under 18 should have parental consent before using the app.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

EU / EEA / UK users have additional rights under GDPR and UK GDPR. Our legal basis for processing is the performance of our contract with you (providing the app's services). To exercise any right, contact us — we respond within 30 days.
