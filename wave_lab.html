01

Introduction

Welcome to Science Lab – Virtual Experiment Suite. Science Lab is an interactive educational app featuring a growing collection of Physics and Chemistry simulations for students and curious minds. Labs currently include Newton's Laws, Ohm's Law, Projectile Motion, AC Circuits, Wave Mechanics, pH & Acid-Base, Atomic & Molecular structures, Electrochemistry, and more — with additional simulations added in future updates.

This Privacy Policy applies to all simulations, features, and content within the Science Lab app, now and in the future. By using Science Lab, you agree to the practices described here.

02

Science Lab is a pure simulation suite. No lab collects, transmits, or stores your experiment inputs — slider values, selected parameters, or results — outside of your device. All simulation data is processed entirely locally.

03

Information We Collect

Science Lab does not collect any personal information. We do not require account registration, and we do not store your name, email address, location, device photos, or any personally identifiable data on our servers.

All simulation inputs and results are handled entirely on your device and are never sent to us.

04

Third-Party Advertising — Google AdMob

The free tier of Science Lab displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain non-personal and device-level information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Ad performance metrics

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy. Users on Pro or Lifetime plans have ads fully removed and AdMob is not active for those sessions.

05

Advertising ID & Ad Personalization

Google AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to display personalized or non-personalized ads. Users aged 15 and above may receive interest-based advertisements.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Upgrading to a Pro or Lifetime plan removes all ads from every lab in the app.

06

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Science Lab offers optional paid plans to unlock the full simulation suite and remove all ads. All transactions are processed securely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store — we never see or store your payment information.

Free users may also watch rewarded ads to gain temporary premium access within a lab session. These are served by Google AdMob under the same data practices described in Section 04.

Subscription and purchase records are managed entirely by your app store. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy or Apple's Privacy Policy for how purchase data is handled on their platforms.

07

Children's Privacy

Science Lab is intended for users aged 15 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

08

Data Retention

Since Science Lab does not collect or store personal data on our end, we have no user data to retain or delete. Subscription status is managed by your app store platform. Any data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's own data retention policies.

09

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect our app and its users. Since we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, the risk of a data breach on our side is minimal. For AdMob-related and purchase-related data, Google and Apple employ industry-standard security measures on their respective platforms.

10

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding data collected by third parties such as Google AdMob — including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and their ad settings to exercise these rights.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as new labs and features are added to Science Lab. Any changes will be reflected with an updated Effective Date at the top of this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically, especially after major app updates that introduce new functionality.
