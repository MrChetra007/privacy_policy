01

Introduction

Welcome to AC Lab — an educational app that lets you explore Ohm's Law through interactive voltage and resistance sliders. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information when you use our app.

By using AC Lab, you agree to the practices described in this document. Please read it carefully.

02

Information We Collect

AC Lab does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require account registration, and we do not store your name, email address, location, photos, or any other personal data on our end.

03

Third-Party Advertising — Google AdMob

AC Lab displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain non-personal and device-level information in order to serve relevant ads, which may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Ad performance metrics

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

04

Advertising ID & Ad Personalization

Google AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized or non-personalized ads. Users aged 15 and above may receive interest-based advertisements.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

05

Children's Privacy

AC Lab is intended for users aged 15 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

06

Data Retention

Since AC Lab does not collect or store personal data, we have no user data to retain or delete. Any data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's own data retention policies.

07

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect our app. Since we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, the risk of a data breach on our side is minimal. For AdMob-related data, Google employs industry-standard security measures.

08

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding data collected by third parties such as Google AdMob — including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and their ad settings to exercise these rights.
