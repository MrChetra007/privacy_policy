Your privacy matters to us. FreelanceFlow is designed to help you manage your freelance business. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work, and we never sell your data.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use FreelanceFlow:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password when you register
  • Business data — client names, project details, invoices, payment records, time entries, and expenses you create within the app
  • Uploaded files — receipt photos and invoice PDFs you upload to the app
  • Profile settings — your currency preference, hourly rate, tax ID, payment terms, and notification preferences
  • Usage data — basic app activity such as feature usage and error logs, to help us improve the app

If you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive your name and email address from Google in accordance with their privacy policy.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FreelanceFlow app
  • Generate invoices, track payments, and calculate your business metrics
  • Send you notifications about overdue payments, upcoming deadlines, and milestones
  • Auto-generate recurring invoices based on your templates
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send you important updates about the app or your account

We do not use your data for advertising purposes and we do not build advertising profiles.

Section 03

Data Storage & Security

FreelanceFlow is powered by Supabase, a secure cloud infrastructure provider. Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database protected by:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) — you can only ever access your own data
  • JWT-based authentication for every request
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) between the app and our servers
  • Private storage buckets for your invoice PDFs and receipt photos

Your financial data, client records, and uploaded files are stored in private buckets. No other user can access your data.

While we take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Section 04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted providers like Supabase who help us operate the app, bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with appropriate notice
  • Your consent — with your explicit permission for any other purpose

When you share an invoice PDF via WhatsApp, Gmail, or Google Drive, that sharing is handled entirely by those third-party apps under their own privacy policies.

Section 05

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and business records within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

Uploaded files (receipts and invoice PDFs) are deleted from our storage immediately upon account deletion.

Section 06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — you can view all your data directly within the app at any time
  • Correction — you can update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen
  • Export — you can contact us to request an export of your data
  • Opt-out — you can disable notifications at any time from the app settings

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at the email below.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

FreelanceFlow is designed for adult freelancers and business owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Your continued use of FreelanceFlow after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

The date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
