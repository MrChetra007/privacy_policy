Request Account Deletion - PawPass

To request deletion of your PawPass account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Open your PawPass app
Step 2: Go to Profile (bottom right)
Step 3: Scroll down and tap "Request Account Deletion"
Step 4: Send an email to sochetra411@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"

Or click below to open your email app:

Send Deletion Request Email

What happens when you request deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All personal data will be removed
  • All pet records, health records, vaccinations, and medications will be deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

Note: Deletion may take up to 30 days. Some data may be retained as required by law.

Alternatively, you can delete your account directly in the app: Profile > Delete Account
